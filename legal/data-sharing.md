# Data Sharing Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]  
**Last Updated:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains how and when information is shared on the Connsura platform.

Connsura operates on a **user-controlled sharing model**: information is never shared unless you authorize it.

## 2. You Control Your Data

Connsura does not sell or rent personal data.

All sharing is:

- Initiated by you  
- Purpose-limited  
- Revocable at any time  
- Logged for transparency and security  

## 3. What Data Can Be Shared

Depending on your choices, shared data may include:

- Contact information  
- Address details  
- Insurance-related profile information  
- Notes included in your profile  

You choose what to share and can revoke access anytime.

## 4. Who Data Is Shared With

### 4.1 User-Selected Recipients

Data is shared only when you select a recipient and grant access.

### 4.2 Responsibility After Sharing

Recipients may retain copies of information you share. Their use of the data is outside Connsura’s control. Share only with trusted parties.

### 4.3 Service Providers

We share limited data with service providers supporting platform operations such as hosting, email delivery, security monitoring, and backup services.

Providers are contractually required to protect data.

## 5. No Advertising or Data Sales

Connsura does not:

- Sell personal data  
- Share data with advertisers  
- Use behavioral advertising  

## 6. Revoking Access

You may revoke access at any time.

When revoked:

- Platform access stops immediately  
- Future updates are not visible  
- Revocation is logged  

Recipients are expected to stop using the data except where retention is required by law.

## 7. Transparency Logs

We maintain logs showing when data was shared, what was shared, and when access was revoked.

## 8. Legal Disclosures

We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.

## 9. Data Minimization

Only data you choose to share is shared. Access is limited and protected.

## 10. Security

Shared data remains protected through encryption, access controls, and monitoring.

## 11. Changes

We may update this policy periodically and will notify users of material changes.
