## 1. Introduction

Connsura (“Connsura”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is an insurance‑technology platform that allows individuals (“Clients”) to securely organize, manage, and share their insurance‑related information with licensed insurance professionals (“Agents”) of their choice. Privacy and data protection are core to our platform design.

This Privacy Policy explains how Connsura collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use our website, services, and related features (collectively, the “Services”).

By using Connsura, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

* Clients creating and managing insurance profiles
* Agents accessing client‑shared data through Connsura
* Visitors to our website
* All web‑based interactions with Connsura (no mobile app at this time)

This Policy does **not** apply to insurance policies themselves, which are governed by the Agent or insurance carrier you choose.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide

Depending on your role, we may collect:

**Clients:**

* Full name, email address, phone number
* Address and location information
* Insurance‑related details (auto, home, renters, commercial, etc.)
* Communication content with Agents

**Agents:**

* Name, business name, license number(s), NPN
* State(s) of licensure
* Contact information and languages spoken
* Profile details and professional credentials

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

We collect limited technical data:

* IP address
* Browser type and device information
* Log data and timestamps
* Security and audit logs

We **do not use tracking ads**, and we do not sell behavioral data.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use information strictly to:

* Provide and operate the Connsura platform
* Enable secure sharing of insurance profiles
* Facilitate communication between Clients and Agents
* Verify Agent licensing and compliance
* Maintain audit logs for security and regulatory purposes
* Prevent fraud and abuse
* Improve system reliability and user experience

We do **not** sell personal data.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Client‑Controlled Sharing

Clients explicitly control when and with whom their data is shared. Agents can only access data when a Client grants permission.

### 5.2 Service Providers

We may share limited data with trusted vendors for:

* Hosting and infrastructure
* Email delivery
* Security monitoring
* Database and backup services

All vendors are contractually required to protect data.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or regulator request.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

Connsura uses industry‑standard security controls including:

* Encrypted data at rest and in transit
* Role‑based access control
* Audit logging and monitoring
* Segregated environments (development, staging, production)
* Secure authentication mechanisms

No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve safeguards.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

* Provide services
* Meet legal, regulatory, and audit requirements
* Resolve disputes

Clients may delete their accounts at any time, subject to retention obligations.
