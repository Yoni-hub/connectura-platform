Connsura (“Connsura”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a technology platform that enables individuals to securely organize, store, and share their insurance-related information.

Protecting your privacy and giving you control over your personal information are core principles of our platform design.

This Privacy Policy explains how Connsura collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use our website and services (the “Services”).

By using Connsura, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- Individuals creating and managing insurance profiles

- Recipients of profile information shared through the platform

This Policy does **not** apply to insurance policies, insurance decisions, or services provided by third parties.

You may provide:

### 5.1 User-Controlled Sharing

You control when and with whom your profile information is shared.
