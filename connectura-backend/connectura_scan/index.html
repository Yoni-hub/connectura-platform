Driver License Scanner

Upload a driver license photo, run OCR locally via open-source Tesseract.js, and extract key details with light heuristics.

Defaults to English; add traineddata to improve other locales.
Phone and PC must be on the same network.
1) Scan QR on phone, upload license.
2) Click this to pull the latest photo and extract.
Open Source: Tesseract.js MIT
